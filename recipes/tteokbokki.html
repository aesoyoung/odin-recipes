Tteokbokki

Description:

This firery dish features chewy rice cakes (garaetteok) seasoned with spicy chile paste.

with sesame seeds for an extra crunch.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups water
  • 2 dried anchovies, or more to taste
  • 3 tablespoons chile paste
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon corn syrup
  • 2 Korean fish cakes, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, thickly sliced
  • 1 spring onion, thickly sliced

Steps:

  1. Combine water and anchovies in a saucepan; bring to a boil and cook for 10 minutes. Remove and discard anchovies; set anchovy water aside.

  2. Combine chile paste, sugar, soy sauce, and corn syrup in a bowl.

  3. Add rice cakes, onion, and chile paste mixture to anchovy water; bring to a boil and boil for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add spring onion; boil 3 minutes more.
